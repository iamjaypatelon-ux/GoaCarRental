Refund & Cancellation Policy

The payment made through this website is a booking confirmation fee.

Customer Cancellation

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before pickup time may be eligible for a refund.

Late Cancellation

Cancellations within 48 hours of pickup may not be eligible for a refund.

Vehicle Unavailability

If the selected vehicle becomes unavailable and no alternative vehicle can be arranged, the booking fee will be fully refunded.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 working days.

Security deposits are handled directly between the customer and the vehicle provider.
